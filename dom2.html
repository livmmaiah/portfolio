Project Summary:
Dom is a crime drama series on Amazon Prime Video, following the story of a father and son. The show focuses on the complicated relationship between a police officer and his son, who is involved in a life of crime. It became the most-watched Brazilian series on Amazon Prime Video, breaking records upon its release and quickly gaining international popularity, particularly in Latin American countries.
