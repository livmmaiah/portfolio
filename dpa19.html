Project Summary:
Detetives do Prédio Azul is a Brazilian children's series that premiered on Gloob in 2012. The show follows three inseparable friends who live in a building, home to witches and other mystical characters. The kids spend their time playing detectives, solving mysteries, and uncovering secrets hidden within the building’s walls.

My Role:
Post-Production Intern Worked closely with directors and the post-production team to ensure smooth communication and workflow. Responsibilities included reviewing episodes to identify and list all special effects, tracking deadlines, updating key post-production documents such as cue sheets, and assisting with the preparation of final deliveries.
